# GOALMOON: Visual Identity & Design System
**Guidelines for UI/UX, Web Design, and Brand Consistency**

## 1. Design Philosophy & Visual Tone
The Goalmoon visual identity must project **sophistication, calm, and cutting-edge technology**. 
* **The Atmosphere:** Deep blue tones dominate the interface to convey modern engineering, authority, and professional rigor. 
* **The Balance:** Lighter, pastel tones are used to create a welcoming, accessible, and user-friendly experience.
* **Layout Principles:** Maintain clean, spacious designs with ample whitespace to avoid visual clutter. The overall architecture must feel modern, elegant, and frictionless.

## 2. Typography
We use a single, highly legible geometric sans-serif typeface to maintain a clean, tech-forward aesthetic.
* **Headings & Highlights (H1-H6):** `Montserrat Bold` – Used for strong, clear presence and structural hierarchy.
* **Body Text & Paragraphs:** `Montserrat Regular` – Ensures high readability and a clean look for long-form content and UI elements.

## 3. Color Palette

### A. Primary Colors (The Tech Core)
* **Deep Navy / Space Blue (`#374161`):** The dominant brand color. Used for primary backgrounds and major UI elements. Conveys professionalism and depth.
* **Dark Slate (`#293047`):** Used for secondary backgrounds, contrasting panels, or dark mode surfaces.
* **Header Blue (`#3F4A6E`):** A dark grayish-blue perfect for subtle highlights, navigation menus, and header accents.
* **Interactive Blues (`#6374AD` & `#879FED`):** Lighter blue tones designated exclusively for interactive elements like text links, secondary buttons, and UI toggles.

### B. Accent & Conversion Colors
* **Action Mint (`#71b399`):** Our primary conversion color. Used sparingly for main Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, focus states, and success indicators. Brings a fresh, accessible touch against the deep blues.

### C. Secondary & Neutral Tones (UI Surfaces)
Used to balance the strong primary colors, providing contrast and soft backgrounds for cards, modals, and content sections.
* **Cool Neutrals:** `#dbe2ea`, `#eaf0f5` (High-contrast light surfaces)
* **Warm Neutrals:** `#E6E0D3`, `#E2DBCD`

### D. Deep Earth & Muted Tones (Contextual)
Used very sparingly for specific visual contexts, data visualization, or dense structural elements like deep footers and sidebars.
* `#562632`, `#856968`, `#FCF7E4`, `#29212C`, `#192639`

## 4. Logo Usage Guidelines
* **Contrast Rules:** Use the logo in `#374161` on light backgrounds. Use the completely `White` logo on dark backgrounds.
* **Integrity:** Never distort the proportions. Do not add artificial effects, outlines, or drop shadows.
* **Safe Space:** Always maintain sufficient padding (clear space) around the logo to ensure maximum visibility and impact, keeping it isolated from dense text or other graphical elements.
